About Frescasa

Frescasa is a renowned Italian company based in Emilia-Romagna, with branches in Palermo, Turin, and Napoli. A family-driven business, it has earned a reputation for blending tradition with innovation. Frescasa specializes in providing high-quality home products that enhance living spaces, combining aesthetics, functionality, and durability. Its legacy of craftsmanship and attention to detail has made it a trusted name in the industry, delivering products that elevate the everyday living experience.

Our mission

Frescasa is dedicated to improving home living by offering premium, sustainable products designed for both beauty and functionality. The company focuses on creating innovative solutions that promote comfort and well-being for its customers, all while maintaining a commitment to eco-friendly practices. By combining traditional craftsmanship with modern design, Frescasa aims to lead the industry toward a greener, more sustainable future.
